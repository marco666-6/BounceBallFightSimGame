ROLE / THEME
------------------------------------------------------------
Yuta is a fast cursed-energy duelist with a short-range katana, a contact
iron-arm hit, an autonomous Rika summon, chain control, and a staged beam
ultimate. He is built as a self-contained exhibition prototype against
DummyBot, matching the current MoroZhar and DarkLord prototype format.

Visual Identity:
- Dark fighter core with bright pink cursed-energy accents.
- Red-wrapped katana, reflective steel iron arm, and white slash after-images.
- Rika is a large pale curse form with black lower body, long arms, claws,
  angular monster face, and moving hair/tendril strips.
- Chain Pull uses separate metallic oval links rather than a plain line.
- Pure Love uses smoke reformation, charge rings, a very wide pink beam,
  white inner beam lines, particles, and target-surface pink fire.


FINALIZED PROTOTYPE STATS
------------------------------------------------------------
Prototype HP: 5000
Intended Tournament HP: To be rebalanced when the shared engine exists.
Defense: Not separately implemented in this prototype.
Movement Speed: Very Fast, approximately 350 pixels per second.
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement.
- Does not naturally chase enemies.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve movement speed.
- Skills, passives, summon logic, and future multi-target selection are
  the only mechanics allowed to aim, redirect, stop, or alter movement.


RESOURCE: CURSE ENERGY
------------------------------------------------------------
Maximum Curse Energy: 750
Starting Curse Energy: 0

Gain rules:
- Yuta gains Curse Energy when his direct attacks deal damage.
- Katana Slash and Iron Arm Punch grant CE based on damage dealt.
- Every Curse Energy gain event receives a flat +2.5 CE bonus.
- Rika Claw grants 22.5 CE before Surge multiplication.
- Pure Love beam hits grant 5 CE per hit.
- Curse Energy Surge can multiply normal CE gain by tier.
- After Rika has spawned and then dies, all of Yuta's Curse Energy gains are
  multiplied by 3 until Rika is revived.
- This death bonus does not activate during Rika's initial 10-second spawn
  delay at the beginning of a round.

Spend Priority:
- Pure Love Blast spends 200 CE when its wall-bounce setup condition is met.
- Curse Energy Surge spends 150, 250, 400, 500, or 600 CE when ready.
- Surge tier selection uses the planned 50/50 highest-affordable vs next
  lower affordable tier rule.
- While Rika is dead after having spawned, automatic Curse Energy Surge is
  held until Yuta has at least 500 CE, preserving energy for a revival tier.


BASIC ATTACKS
------------------------------------------------------------
Yuta has two independent basic attack checks. If both are available on the
same frame, Katana Slash resolves first because it is his primary weapon,
then Iron Arm Punch may resolve if Yuta is also colliding with the target.

Basic Attack 1: Katana Slash
Range: Short range, approximately 175 pixels from Yuta's surface
Interval: 0.40 seconds while an enemy is in range
Damage: 35 base damage per successful slash
Attack Behavior:
- Yuta draws a red-wrapped katana slash arc over the enemy.
- The attack does not require physical contact.
- Slash animations alternate sides and spawn impact sparks.
- Each successful hit heals Yuta for 25% of the basic attack damage dealt.
- Basic attacks do not redirect or stop Yuta's movement.
- At higher enhancement tiers, Katana Slash can spawn after-image follow-up
  hits at 0.3 second spacing.

Basic Attack 2: Iron Arm Punch
Range: Contact
Interval: 0.45 seconds while colliding with an enemy
Damage: 45 base damage per successful punch
Behavior:
- Yuta punches with a large reflective iron arm and fist.
- The attack requires physical contact.
- The animation follows the same readability style as MoroZhar's punch,
  but with a steel prosthetic/armored design.
- Each successful hit heals Yuta for 25% of the basic attack damage dealt.
- Basic attacks do not redirect or stop Yuta's movement.
- Higher Surge tiers can add Stun and Chain Pull.


SKILL 1: CURSE ENERGY SURGE
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 10 seconds after the active period ends
Active Duration: 10 seconds
Requirement: At least 150 Curse Energy
Spend Rule:
- When cast, Yuta consumes Curse Energy with a 50/50 chance between the
  highest affordable tier and the next highest affordable tier below it
  (600, 500, 400, 250, or 150).
- If no lower affordable tier exists, he uses the lowest affordable tier.
- The tier is snapshotted for the full 10-second active period.
- If cooldown is ready but Curse Energy is below 150, the skill waits.
- Cooldown does not begin until the 10-second active period finishes.

Tier 5:
- Cost: 150 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.25x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.25x
- No extra control effect.

Tier 4:
- Cost: 250 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.40x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.25x
- Katana Slash has a 35% chance to trigger Double Slash.
- Double Slash consists of the original Katana Slash and an after-image
  follow-up strike.
- Iron Arm Punch has a 25% chance to immobilize/stun the target for 2 seconds.

Tier 3:
- Cost: 400 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.60x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.40x
- Katana Slash has a 35% chance to trigger Triple Slash.
- Triple Slash consists of the original Katana Slash and two after-image
  follow-up strikes.
- Iron Arm Punch has a 35% chance to stun the target for 3 seconds.
- Stunned enemies show a rotating overhead dizzy indicator.

Tier 2:
- Cost: 500 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.60x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.50x
- Every Katana Slash has a 100% chance to trigger Triple Slash.
- Iron Arm Punch has a 35% chance to stun the target for 2 seconds and apply
  Chain Pull for 5 seconds.
- Chain Pull holds the enemy near Yuta so Katana Slash can keep reaching them.
- If Rika has died, revive Rika at half HP near Yuta after smoke reformation.
- Rika's saved Pure Love cooldown state is preserved when revived.

Tier 1:
- Cost: 600 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.80x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.50x
- Every Katana Slash has a 100% chance to trigger Triple Slash.
- Iron Arm Punch has a 45% chance to stun the target for 3 seconds and apply
  Chain Pull for 6 seconds.
- If Rika is alive, heal Rika to full HP.
- If Rika has died, revive Rika at full HP near Yuta after smoke reformation.
- Rika's saved Pure Love cooldown state is preserved when revived.

Finalized Presentation:
- Surge creates a violet cursed-energy burst and ring around Yuta.
- HUD displays active tier and duration.
- The 13 supplied sound effects are mapped and used directly.


SKILL 2: PURE LOVE BEAM BLAST
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 30 seconds after cast

Requirements:
- At least 200 Curse Energy
- Rika has to be alive
- Yuta must wall-bounce while the cooldown is ready
- Yuta must not already be locked in another staged action

Cost: 200 Curse Energy

Range / Area:
- Straight horizontal beam from the nearest arena side to the opposite wall.
- The beam stops at the arena wall and does not stop on enemy contact.
- The beam is intentionally very wide, approximately triple MoroZhar's
  Heat Vision visual width.

Cast Rules:
- Activation: On a valid wall-bounce, Rika disappears using smoke while Yuta
  is redirected to the vertical center of the nearest left or right edge.
- Setup: Rika reforms directly in front of Yuta after 1.25 seconds.
- Charge: Yuta and Rika charge together for 1.5 seconds.
- Blast: Pure Love fires for 7 seconds.
- Rika remains a collidable body while manifested during the charge and blast
  phases.
- Rika is immune to damage during Pure Love charge and blast. Enemy hits on
  Rika create a pink shield-pop ripple instead of reducing her HP.
- Recovery: Rika disappears in smoke, returns to the location she was
  teleported from, reforms, and Yuta resumes movement in a random direction.

Beam Effects:
- Damage: 40 per hit
- Damage Interval: 0.25 seconds
- Each successful beam hit heals Yuta for 10 HP, up to maximum HP.
- Each successful beam hit grants Yuta 2.5 CE, up to maximum CE.

Aim / Collision Behavior:
- Does not auto-aim.
- Fires straight left or right depending on Yuta's standing edge.
- Does not stop at target contact.
- Hit enemies show pink fire and beam particles.
- Never passes through arena walls.
- The beam is a cone/funnel. It starts as a narrow point at a charge ball in
  front of Rika and widens until it spans the full opposite wall.
- Enemies inside the cone are steadily pushed toward the far wall while they
  remain inside the beam.
- Yuta stays stationed behind Rika during the Pure Love sequence.
- While stationed behind Rika, Yuta can still use Katana Slash or Iron Arm
  Punch during Pure Love charge and blast if the enemy reaches him and is
  outside the beam cone.

Finalized Presentation:
- Rika reforms in front of Yuta with pale smoke.
- Charge phase uses a visible curse-energy charge ball, pink rings, smoke,
  inward particles, and pink electricity.
- Blast phase uses a triangular/funnel beam with jagged pink electric edges,
  white inner streaks, smoke, particles, and pink fire-on-hit visuals.


PASSIVE / SUMMON: RIKA
------------------------------------------------------------
Spawn Delay: 10 seconds after round start
Spawn Position: Near Yuta, offset away from the nearest wall when possible
Size: Approximately 2x a normal fighter body
HP: 450
Movement Speed: Medium-Fast, approximately 280 pixels per second
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement, same baseline style as characters.
- Does not naturally chase enemies.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve movement speed.
- Every Rika wall bounce redirects her toward the nearest hostile body.
- If an enemy summon is closer than its owner, Rika redirects to that summon.
- This redirect preserves Rika's current speed and adds no speed boost.
- Rika never attacks Yuta.
- Rika can be damaged by DummyBot's contact punch.
- Rika's HP bar is a thin bar above her body.

Rika Basic Attacks: Claw/Scratch
Range: Short range, approximately 145 pixels from Rika's surface
Interval: 0.5 seconds while an enemy is in range
Damage: 30 per successful hit

Effects:
- Each successful Claw/Scratch grants Yuta +22.5 CE before Surge multiplication.
- Each successful Claw/Scratch heals Rika for 15 HP, half of her base claw
  damage, up to her maximum HP.
- Claw/Scratch moves Rika's arms/hands toward the enemy and draws animated
  scratch marks over the target.
- Rika's body has animated hair/tendrils and long monster arms.

Rika Passive:
- Summoning Rika is Yuta's character passive.
- As long as Rika is alive, every second Yuta wall bounce redirects Yuta
  toward the nearest hostile body.
- If an enemy summon is closer than its owner, Yuta redirects to that summon.
- Yuta and Rika wall redirects preserve current speed and add no speed boost.
- If Rika dies after spawning, Yuta gains Curse Energy at 3x speed and will
  reserve at least 500 CE before automatically casting Curse Energy Surge.
- The 3x gain ends immediately when Rika is revived.
- Rika can be revived or restored by high-tier Curse Energy Surge.
- Pure Love temporarily relocates Rika but preserves her current HP.

Finalized Presentation:
- Pale monster torso, black tail/lower body, long arms, claws, open mouth,
  teeth, and moving hair/tendril strips.
- Spawn/revive/teleport-in use smoke and a growth-from-small reformation.
- Death/teleport-out use smoke, floor-sink, shrink, and melt-style collapse.


MULTI-ENEMY TARGETING / LOCK-ON PLAN
------------------------------------------------------------
This is the planned mechanism for the future shared engine when there are
summons.

Definitions:
- A "combatant" is a damageable character body.
- A "summon" is a damageable allied helper body owned by a combatant.
- Each team has an owner group: for example Yuta and Rika are one allied group.
- Fighters and summons never target their own owner group.

Threat Target Selection:
- Prefer the enemy combatant unless a summon is closer and is allowed to be
  damaged by the current action.
- Basic attacks can use nearest valid hostile body.
- Major staged skills may snapshot the combatant target or arena direction.

Lock Strength:
- Soft Lock: Normal target preference for basic attacks, beams, and copied
  skills. Soft lock can retarget anytime a better target is chosen.
- Hard Lock: Temporary forced relation created by mechanics such as
  DarkLord's double-stab lock or Yuta's Chain Pull. Hard lock overrides
  normal retargeting until the effect ends or either actor dies.
- Snapshot Aim: Projectiles, dashes, and beam casts may snapshot a target's
  current or last-known position. Snapshot aim does not keep tracking unless
  the skill specifically says it homes or auto-aims.

Yuta / Rika Targeting:
- Yuta and Rika are the same owner group.
- Rika never targets or damages Yuta.
- Chain Pull is a Hard Lock while active.


STATUS DEFINITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
Stun:
- Prevents normal movement and starting new attacks, skills, passive actions,
  or other actions.
- Already-deployed actions and effects continue and finish normally.

Immobilize:
- Prevents normal movement only.
- Attacks, skills, passive actions, and cooldowns continue normally.
- Already-deployed actions and forced movement continue and finish normally.

Chain Pull:
- Prevents the chained target from moving farther than the chain length
  from Yuta.
- Current chain length: approximately 120 pixels between body surfaces.
- Does not automatically prevent attacking or casting.
- Counts as a Hard Lock while active.
