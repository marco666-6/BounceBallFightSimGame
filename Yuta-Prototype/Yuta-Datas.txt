BASIC ATTACKS
------------------------------------------------------------
Yuta has two independent basic attack checks. If both are available on the
same frame, Katana Slash resolves first because it is his primary weapon,
then Iron Arm Punch may resolve if Yuta is also colliding with the target.

Basic Attack 1: Katana Slash
Range: Short range, approximately 130 pixels from Yuta's surface
Interval: 0.45 seconds while an enemy is in range
Damage: ? per successful slash
Attack Behavior:
- Yuta pulls out his katana and slashes the enemy.
- The attack does not require physical contact.
- The Katana reaches toward the target and may alternate between left,
  right, overhead, and crossing slash animations.
- Basic attacks do not redirect or stop Yuta's movement.
- At higher enhancement tiers, a single katana slash can generate an after-image follow-up strike 0.3 seconds later (referred to as a *Double Slash*), 
  and at even higher tiers, a second after-image strike may occur another 0.3 seconds after the first after-image follow-up strike (referred to as a *Triple Slash*).

Basic Attack 2: Iron Arm Punch
Range: Contact
Interval: 0.5 seconds while colliding with an enemy
Damage: ? per successful punch
Behavior:
- Yuta punch the enemy with his iron arm.
- The attack does require physical contact.
- The punch animation and concept are the same as MoroZhar’s punches, except for the different hand/arm design.
- Basic attacks do not redirect or stop Yuta's movement.
- At higher enhancement tiers, an iron arm punch may apply an immobilize/stun effect on you for 2 seconds, 
  and at even higher tiers, an Iron Arm punch may apply a Chain Pull effect, restricting the target’s movement to the chain’s range without preventing attacks or skill casting.


SKILL 1: CURSE ENERGY SURGE
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 10 seconds after the active period ends
Active Duration: 10 seconds
Requirement: At least 150 Curse Energy
Spend Rule:
- When cast, Yuta consumes Curse Energy with a 50/50 chance between the highest affordable tier and the next highest affordable tier below it 
  (600, 500, 400, 250, or 150); if no lower affordable tier exists, means he automatically uses the lowest affordable tier.
- The tier is snapshotted for the full 10-second active period.
- If cooldown is ready but Curse Energy is below 150, the skill waits.
- Cooldown does not begin until the 10-second active period finishes.

Tier 5: 
- Cost: 150 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.25x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.25x
- No extra control effect.

Tier 4: 
- Cost: 250 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.40x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.25x
- Katana Slash has a 35% chance to trigger Double Slash.
- Double Slash consists of the original Katana Slash and an after-image follow-up strike, 
  with each hit dealing 1.40× the original Katana Slash's base damage (totaling 2.8× base damage if both hits connect).
- Iron Arm Punch has a 25% chance to Immobilize the target for 2 seconds.

Tier 3:

- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.60x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.40x
- Katana Slash has a 35% chance to trigger Triple Slash.
- Triple Slash consists of the original Katana Slash, an after-image follow-up strike, and another after-image follow-up strike, 
  with each hit dealing 1.60× the original Katana Slash's base damage (totaling 4.8× base damage if all hits connect).
- Iron Arm Punch has a 35% chance to Stun the target for 3 seconds.
- Stunned enemies show a dizzy/bird-style overhead indicator.

Tier 2:
- Cost: 500 Curse Energy
- Basic attack damage multiplier: 1.60x
- Curse energy gain multiplier: 1.50x
- Every Katana Slash has a 100% chance to trigger Triple Slash.
- Triple Slash consists of the original Katana Slash, an after-image follow-up strike, and another after-image follow-up strike, 
  with each hit dealing 1.60× the original Katana Slash's base damage (totaling 4.8× base damage if all hits connect).

- While chained, the enemy is pulled/held to approximately 130 pixels from Yuta's surface so Katana Slash can keep reaching them.
- Chain Pull is a forced-position relation, not a full Stun by itself. If Stun expires while the chain remains,

- Rika's saved cooldown state is preserved when revived. For example, if Pure Love had 7 seconds remaining when Rika died,

SKILL 2: "PURE LOVE" BEAM BLAST
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 20 seconds after cast

Requirements:
- At least 200 Curse Energy
- Rika has to be alive
- 
- 

Cost: 200 Curse Energy

Range / Area: Just like MoroZhar’s Heat Vision. Straight beam that travels until colliding with the arena wall (doesn't stop on enemy like MoroZhar’s), and is 3 times wider than MoroZhar's Heat Vision.

Cast Rules:
- Activation: When the cooldown is ready, Rika is alive, and Yuta has sufficient Curse Energy, if Yuta does a wall-bounce, Rika disappears using a 1.25-second reverse curse-smoke reformation animation. 
  On that same bounce, Yuta is redirected to the vertical center of the nearest arena edge (far left or far right) and enters a standing state (not moving and stays there).
- Setup & Charge: Once Yuta reaches the position, Rika reforms directly in front of him with a 1.25-second curse-smoke reformation animation. Yuta and Rika then begin charging together for 1.5 seconds.
- Pure Love Blast: After charging, they unleash “Pure Love”, a beam attack lasting 7 seconds. Once the attack ends, Rika disappears again using the reverse curse-smoke reformation animation 
  and returns to the location she was teleported from, while Yuta resumes movement from his standing position in a random direction.
- 
- 

Beam Effects:
- Damage: 40 per hit
- Damage Interval: 0.25 seconds
- Each successful Pure Love beam hit heals Yuta for 10 HP, up to maximum HP.
- Each successful Pure Love beam hit collects for Yuta curse energy for 2.5 CE, up to maximum CE.

Aim / Collision Behavior:
- Does not auto-aims toward the target it just aims straight to the left or right side depends on where his standing point is,

MULTI-ENEMY TARGETING / LOCK-ON PLAN
------------------------------------------------------------
This is the planned mechanism for the future shared engine when there are summons exist.

Definitions:
- A "combatant" is a damageable character body.
- A "summon" is a damageable allied helper body owned by a combatant.
- Each team has an owner group: for example Yuta and Rika are one allied group.
- Fighters and summons never target their own owner group.

Threat Target Selection:
- 
- 
- 

Lock Strength:
- Soft Lock: Normal target preference for basic attacks, beams, and copied
  skills. Soft lock can retarget anytime a better target is chosen.
- Hard Lock: Temporary forced relation created by mechanics such as
  DarkLord's double-stab lock or Yuta's Chain Pull. Hard lock overrides
  normal retargeting until the effect ends or either actor dies.
- Snapshot Aim: Projectiles, dashes, and beam casts may snapshot a target's
  current or last-known position. Snapshot aim does not keep tracking unless
  the skill specifically says it homes or auto-aims.

STATUS DEFINITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
Stun:
- Prevents normal movement and starting new attacks, skills, passive actions,
  or other actions.
- Already-deployed actions and effects continue and finish normally.

Immobilize:
- Prevents normal movement only.
- Attacks, skills, passive actions, and cooldowns continue normally.
- Already-deployed actions and forced movement continue and finish normally.

Chain Pull:
- Prevents the chained target from moving farther than the chain length
  from Yuta.
- Does not automatically prevent attacking or casting.
- Counts as a Hard Lock while active.
