============================================================
CHARACTER: DUMMYBOT
PROTOTYPE STATUS: COMPLETE
============================================================

ROLE / THEME
------------------------------------------------------------
DummyBot is a simple grey sparring opponent used to validate character
prototypes. It has no offensive skills and exists to test contact
combat, movement, status effects, particles, audio, and passive logic.

Visual Identity:
- Grey 3D ball with metallic shading and simple eyes.
- Reacts visibly to hits, burn, slow, freeze, and other effects.


FINALIZED PROTOTYPE STATS
------------------------------------------------------------
Prototype HP: 10000
Intended Tournament HP: To be rebalanced when the shared engine exists.
Defense: Not separately implemented in this prototype.
Movement Speed: Low-Medium, approximately 212 pixels per second.
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement.
- Does not naturally chase or flee from enemies.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve movement speed.
- Slow reduces movement speed to 25%.
- Freeze completely stops movement.


BASIC ATTACK
------------------------------------------------------------
Attack Name: Punch
Range: Contact
Interval: 1 second while colliding with an enemy
Damage: 255
Effects:
- Deals direct physical damage only.
- Does not apply knockback or alter movement speed.
- Creates a simple blocky mechanical fist animation from a randomized
  incoming direction.
- The fist quickly passes through the target with muted speed streaks
  and sparse dust particles.


SKILL 1
------------------------------------------------------------
None


SKILL 2
------------------------------------------------------------
None


PASSIVE: OH GOD HELP ME
------------------------------------------------------------
Recharge: 10 seconds
Charges: 3 redirected wall bounces per recharge
Effect:
- When DummyBot wall-bounces with charges available, its movement
  redirects away from the enemy's last known position.
- Redirection preserves DummyBot's constant movement speed.
- Deals no damage.


SUPPORTED STATUS SYSTEMS
------------------------------------------------------------
DummyBot currently supports these shared prototype combat states:
- Ice Stacks
- Heat Stacks and timed Heat Stack decay of one stack every 4.5 seconds
- Freeze and increased frozen damage
- Stun
- Slow
- Burn damage over time
- Thermal Shock trigger locking
- Hit reaction, squash, status particles, and floating damage text


FINALIZED AUDIO
------------------------------------------------------------
- DummyBot currently uses DummyBot-SoundEffects/MoroZhar-Punch.mp3
  as its temporary punch sound.


PROTOTYPE NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------
- DummyBot.py is a reusable sparring class.
- Its body renderer, movement presentation, and punch animation are shared
  by completed character prototypes through Prototype-Shared/visuals.py.
- Run any completed character prototype to watch a matchup.
- DummyBot is intentionally simple so each new character's mechanics
  remain the focus of its prototype.
