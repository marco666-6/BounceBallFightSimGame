ROLE / THEME
------------------------------------------------------------
Naruto is a high-speed swarm and pressure fighter. He uses
Kagebunshin No Jutsu to flood the arena with autonomous clones, keeps
pressure active through close-range Ninja Punches and long-range
Shuriken Throws, and threatens huge momentum swings with Rasengan.

He is strongest when several clones are alive and bouncing around the
arena. His Keikaku passive stops him in place, makes him invulnerable,
and refills Chakra in visible timed pulses so he can afford clone waves
and Rasengan again.

Visual Identity:
- Bright orange 3D ball with gold accent, headband plate, and whisker
  marks.
- Blue-white Chakra aura grows brighter as Chakra rises.
- Clones are pale/desaturated orange Naruto copies with individual HP
  bars.
- Keikaku draws smooth inward-flowing Chakra particles with a small
  pulsing blue-white aura around Naruto.
- Rasengan draws a dense blue-white spinning sphere near Naruto's
  forward side, then a heavy impact burst with spiral energy, smoke,
  orange heat wisps, chakra shards, sparks, and screen shake.
- Ninja Punch draws a large animated orange/chakra fist from a random
  incoming direction.
- Shuriken Throw creates visible spinning shuriken projectiles with
  small metallic trails.


FINALIZED PROTOTYPE STATS
------------------------------------------------------------
Prototype HP: 5000
Prototype Opponent: DummyBot, 10000 HP
Defense: Not separately implemented in this standalone prototype.
Movement Speed: 350 pixels per second.
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve normal movement speed.
- Naruto and his Clones physically collide and bounce off each other.
- Naruto does not naturally chase the enemy.
- Clones use DVD movement too, but every second wall bounce redirects
  them toward DummyBot.
- Skills, passives, projectile aim, and clone redirect logic are the
  only mechanics that intentionally aim, stop, or alter movement.


RESOURCE: CHAKRA
------------------------------------------------------------
Maximum Chakra: 1000
Starting Chakra: 100

Gain Rules:
- Naruto's successful Ninja Punch grants Naruto 15 Chakra.
- Naruto's successful Shuriken Throw grants Naruto 10 Chakra.
- Clone attacks do not grant Naruto Chakra.
- Keikaku grants 45 Chakra every 0.5 seconds during its 3-second
  active period, for up to 270 Chakra total.
- Chakra cannot exceed 1000.

Spend Priority:
1. Kagebunshin No Jutsu
   - Cost: 300 Chakra.
   - Highest priority when off cooldown and allowed by clone count.

2. Rasengan
   - Cost: 500 Chakra.
   - Cast when off cooldown and Naruto has enough Chakra, but only
     after Kagebunshin priority is checked.

3. Keikaku
   - No Chakra cost.
   - Triggers automatically when off cooldown and Chakra is below max.
   - If Chakra is already max when the check happens, the cooldown
     restarts instead of wasting the passive.


BASIC ATTACKS
------------------------------------------------------------
Basic Attack 1: Ninja Punch
Range: 120 pixels beyond body contact.
Interval: 0.20 seconds.
Damage: 30 per hit.

Implemented Behavior:
- Fires when DummyBot is inside short range.
- Creates a big animated fist from a randomized incoming direction.
- Naruto gains 15 Chakra on his own successful punches.
- The attacker heals for 45% of damage dealt.
- Clones use 48% of Naruto's damage:
  - Clone Ninja Punch: 14 damage
  - Clone Shuriken Throw: 9 damage
- Clone hits heal that clone and do not grant Naruto Chakra.
- Clone healing automatically scales down because lifesteal is based on
  damage dealt.

Basic Attack 2: Shuriken Throw
Range: 500 pixels beyond body contact.
Interval: 0.40 seconds.
Damage: 20 per hit.

Implemented Behavior:
- Fires only when DummyBot is outside Ninja Punch range but inside
  Shuriken range.
- Spawns a visible spinning shuriken projectile.
- Projectile travels in a straight line to the snapshotted target
  direction and does not home.
- Projectile disappears when it hits DummyBot or leaves the arena.
- Naruto gains 10 Chakra on his own successful shuriken hits.
- The attacker heals for 45% of damage dealt.
- Three throw sound variants are randomly selected.


SKILL 1: KAGEBUNSHIN NO JUTSU
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 12 seconds.
Requirement: 300 Chakra.
Normal Cost: 300 Chakra.

Clone Cap Rule:
- Maximum active clones: 6.
- Normal cast creates 3 clones.
- If exactly 4 clones are alive, the cast creates 2 clones instead so
  the cap is respected.
- If 5 or 6 clones are alive and Naruto tries to cast, Chakra Fail
  Control triggers instead.

Chakra Fail Control:
- Duration: 1.5 seconds.
- Stuns Naruto and all active clones.
- Does not spend Chakra.
- Damages each active clone by 100 HP.
- Kagebunshin cooldown begins after the stun ends.
- Visuals: erratic blue-white sparks, big text label, and impact burst.

Each Clone:
- HP: 70.
- Radius: same as Naruto.
- Speed: 350 pixels per second.
- Has its own thin HP bar.
- Can be hit and killed by DummyBot contact punches.
- Uses 48%-damage versions of Naruto's Ninja Punch and Shuriken Throw.
- Cannot cast Kagebunshin, Rasengan, or Keikaku.
- Does not gain or store Chakra.
- On spawn: white smoke puff and grow-in animation.
- On death: white smoke/poof effect and short disappearing body.


SKILL 2: RASENGAN
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 7.5 seconds.
Requirement: 500 Chakra.
Cost: 500 Chakra.

Charge Behavior:
- Naruto begins a 0.6-second visual charge.
- Naruto keeps moving during the charge.
- After charge completes, Rasengan remains ready until Naruto collides
  with DummyBot.
- The skill uses natural body contact; it does not snap aim or dash.

On Contact:
- Damage: 120.
- Creates a large blue-white spiral explosion.
- Applies strong hit-stop and camera shake.
- Applies massive knockback to DummyBot away from Naruto.
- Knockback starts at about 1400 pixels per second and rapidly decays.
- If DummyBot hits an arena wall during this forced knockback, DummyBot
  is stunned for 2 seconds.
- Wall impact plays its own sound and draws crack/burst visuals.


PASSIVE: KEIKAKU (CHAKRA FILL)
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 6 seconds after the active period ends.
Active Duration: 3 seconds.

Trigger Condition:
- Activates automatically when cooldown is ready and Naruto's Chakra
  is below 1000.
- If Chakra is already full, the cooldown restarts and Naruto remains
  mobile.

Active Period:
- Naruto stops moving.
- Naruto cannot use basic attacks, Kagebunshin, or Rasengan.
- Naruto is invulnerable in the same way Rika is during Pure Love
  Blast: collisions, attacks, and skills can still connect, but they
  deal zero damage and create a Chakra shield ripple instead.
- Every 0.5 seconds Naruto gains 45 Chakra, up to max.
- The period always completes once it starts.

Visuals:
- Inward-flowing blue-white Chakra particles.
- A small smooth blue-white circle aura around Naruto.
- Floating CHAKRA FILL text.
- Each gain tick creates a pulse burst, sparks, and a +Chakra label.

PROTOTYPE NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------
- Combat is fully autonomous.
- This prototype intentionally targets DummyBot only. The future
  tournament adapter should replace target lookup with shared hostile
  body selection for enemy summons and multi-combatant cases.
- Clones currently collide with Naruto, one another, and DummyBot to
  keep the swarm physically readable.
- Rasengan contact is body collision based, matching the intended
  natural DVD-movement requirement.
- Keikaku invulnerability is implemented locally as zero incoming
  damage plus shield ripple feedback.
- The shared arena and shared prototype helpers were not modified.
