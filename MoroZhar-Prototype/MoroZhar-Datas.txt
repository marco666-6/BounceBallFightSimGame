PROTOTYPE STATUS: COMPLETE

ROLE / THEME
------------------------------------------------------------
MoroZhar is a medium-speed pressure fighter who controls extreme
cold and heat. He builds elemental stacks through contact attacks,
then converts them into freeze control, burn damage, Thermal Shock,
or increasingly powerful Heat Vision casts.

Visual Identity:
- Dark blue 3D ball with separate ice-blue and heat-red detailing.
- The next queued punch is always shown through a colored rotating
  aura, overhead label, and HUD label.
- Every successful punch creates a large fading animated fist that
  strikes the enemy from a randomized incoming direction.
- The fist quickly passes completely through the target instead of
  stopping at the impact point.
- Normal Punch is metallic, Ice Punch uses icy shards and frost
  particles, and Heat Punch uses fiery particles and embers.
- Ice, heat, stack, status, cooldown, and damage effects are visible.


FINALIZED PROTOTYPE STATS
------------------------------------------------------------
Prototype HP: 5000
Intended Tournament HP: To be rebalanced when the shared engine exists.
Defense: Not separately implemented in this prototype.
Movement Speed: Medium-Fast, approximately 280 pixels per second.
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement.
- Does not naturally chase enemies.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve movement speed.
- Skills and passives are the only mechanics allowed to aim,
  redirect, stop, or alter movement.


BASIC ATTACK
------------------------------------------------------------
Attack Names: Punch / Ice Punch / Heat Punch
Range: Contact
Interval: 0.5 seconds while colliding with an enemy

Attack Selection:
- 10% Normal Punch
- 35% Ice Punch
- 55% Heat Punch
- The next selected punch is visibly telegraphed before contact.

Damage:
- Normal Punch: 150
- Ice Punch: 60 and applies 1 Ice Stack
- Heat Punch: 65, applies 1 Heat Stack, and heals MoroZhar for 12 HP

Ice Stacks:
- Maximum: 3
- Reaching 3 stacks primes the target but does not immediately Freeze it.
- Applying another Ice Stack while the target is already at 3 stacks
  Freezes the target for 5 seconds.
- Ice Stacks reset to 0 when Freeze activates.
- Frozen targets take 10% increased damage.
- When MoroZhar successfully Freezes the enemy, he heals for 100 HP,
  up to maximum HP.

Heat Stacks:
- Maximum: 3
- At 3 stacks, the target is Burned for 7 seconds.
- Heat Stacks remain stored after Burn activates.
- Stored stacks decay one at a time every 4.5 seconds without
  additional heat application.
- Stored stacks are consumed when Heat Vision is cast.

Burn:
- Duration: 7 seconds
- Damage: 32 every 0.5 seconds
- Each Burn damage tick heals MoroZhar for 7 HP, up to maximum HP.

Thermal Shock:
- Activates once when the target has both Ice and Heat Stacks.
- Damage: 50
- Does not consume either stack type.
- Cannot repeatedly trigger from the same unchanged mixed-stack state.


SKILL 1: FREEZING BREATH
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 7.5 seconds, beginning immediately after cast
Range / Area: Medium forward cone, approximately 430 pixels
Aim Behavior:
- Casts only when the enemy is inside the forward cone.
- Fires in MoroZhar's current movement direction.
- Does not auto-aim or chase the target.

Effects:
- Initial Damage: 150
- Applies 2 Ice Stacks
- Slows movement by 75% for 3 seconds
- Can activate Freeze when hitting a target already at 3 Ice Stacks

Finalized Presentation:
- No visible filled cone.
- Directional frost wind, mist, and ice particles fill the entire
  cone-shaped gameplay area.
- Frozen enemies are surrounded by an irregular, opaque ice prison
  with snow crust, frozen chunks, jagged growths, and icicles.
- At 2 seconds before thawing, visible cracks begin forming and
  MoroZhar-IceBreakForEnemy.mp3 plays.
- When Freeze ends, the ice prison shatters and the enemy resumes
  movement.
- Freeze prevents normal movement and starting new attacks, skills, passive
  actions, or other actions. Anything already deployed, including Heat Vision
  and Freezing Breath, continues and finishes normally.


SKILL 2: HEAT VISION
------------------------------------------------------------
Base Cooldown: 7.5 seconds after the beam period ends
Requirement: At least 1 stored Heat Stack
Range / Area: Straight beam ending at the first enemy hit or arena wall

Cast-Level Rules:
- Stored stacks are snapshotted and consumed when cast.
- Level 1: 1 stored stack, 2.0-second beam
- Level 2: 2 stored stacks, 3.5-second beam
- Level 3: 3 stored stacks, 7.0-second beam
- MoroZhar may briefly wait for stronger stacks, but Heat Stack decay
  prevents permanent Level 3 loops.

Beam Effects:
- Damage: 40 per hit
- Damage Interval: 0.35 seconds
- Each successful Heat Vision hit heals MoroZhar for 12 HP, up to maximum HP.
- Applies 1 Heat Stack every 2 successful hits
- Can activate Burn
- Can activate Thermal Shock against mixed Ice and Heat Stacks
- Frozen targets take the standard 10% increased damage

Aim / Collision Behavior:
- Auto-aims toward the target at approximately 1.75 radians
  per second, allowing the target to evade it.
- Stops exactly at the target surface when it hits.
- Stops exactly at the arena wall when it misses.
- Never passes through targets or arena walls.


PASSIVE: REDIRECTION
------------------------------------------------------------
Recharge: 10 seconds
Charges: 3 redirected wall bounces per recharge
Effect:
- When MoroZhar wall-bounces with charges available, his movement
  direction redirects toward the enemy's last known position.
- Redirection increases MoroZhar's movement speed to 1.35x for 2.5 seconds.
- Redirecting again during the boost refreshes its duration without stacking.
- When the boost expires, MoroZhar returns to his normal movement speed.
- Deals no damage.

PROTOTYPE NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------
- Combat is fully autonomous.
- Fullscreen flashing effects are intentionally disabled.
- Effects use localized particles, glow, impact frames, hit-stop,
  ball squash, and screen shake.
- MoroZhar.py currently contains the standalone battle showcase and
  imports DummyBot.py as its opponent.
- The arena, fonts, and DummyBot punch presentation now use
  Prototype-Shared/visuals.py so later prototypes preserve the same style.
- MoroZhar has a registered adapter in the root main.py tournament runtime
  and can autonomously fight other registered eligible characters.
