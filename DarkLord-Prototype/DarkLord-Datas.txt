PROTOTYPE STATUS: COMPLETE
============================================================

ROLE / THEME
------------------------------------------------------------
DarkLord is a very fast, short-range pressure and arena-control
fighter who uses the Vira-Band to create weapons, portals, creatures,
and unstable Vira-Liquid. His rapid Vira-Blade attacks gradually seed
the arena with liquid hazards before every tenth Vira Stack causes all
active liquid pools to harden and erupt into dangerous red-edged spikes.

DarkLord is strongest when his movement repeatedly carries him near the
enemy. His wall teleport gives him an occasional aimed engage, while
Dark Energy Fire Ball and roaming Vira-Spiders let him apply pressure
when his normal movement path does not reach the enemy.

Visual Identity:
- Bright red 3D ball inspired by The Dark Lord's red stick-figure body,
  with a darker red shadow side, an intense red glow, sharp eyes, and
  visible black-and-red Vira-Bands worn around both sides of the body.
- Vira constructs are primarily black liquid or hardened black matter
  with vivid red outlines, inner red cracks, and smoky red particles.
- Vira-Liquid pools have irregular, slightly animated silhouettes so
  repeated pools do not look identical.
- Liquid constructs melt back into fluid and vaporize into dark smoke.
- Hardened constructs form quickly with sharp, aggressive movements.
- Important attacks use localized glow, hit-stop, squash, speed streaks,
  particles, and restrained screen shake without fullscreen flashes.


FINALIZED PROTOTYPE STATS
------------------------------------------------------------
Prototype HP: 5000
Intended Tournament HP: To be rebalanced when the shared engine exists.
Defense: Not separately implemented in this prototype.
Movement Speed: Very Fast, approximately 350 pixels per second.
Movement Behavior:
- Constant-speed DVD-style movement.
- Does not naturally chase enemies.
- Wall and fighter collisions preserve movement speed.
- Skills and passives are the only mechanics allowed to aim, redirect,
  stop, or alter movement.


BASIC ATTACK: VIRA-BLADE SLASH
------------------------------------------------------------
Attack Name: Vira-Blade Slash
Range: Short range, approximately 125 pixels from DarkLord's surface
Interval: 0.35 seconds while an enemy is in range
Damage: 32 per successful slash

Attack Behavior:
- DarkLord forms a Vira-Blade from the Vira-Band and slashes the enemy.
- The attack does not require physical contact.
- The blade reaches toward the target and may alternate between left,
  right, overhead, and crossing slash animations.
- Basic attacks do not redirect or stop DarkLord's movement.

Every Two Successful Basic Attack Hits:
- Creates one irregular Vira-Liquid pool centered on the enemy's
  position at the moment of the second hit.
- Grants DarkLord 1 Vira Stack.
- The two-hit counter counts successful Vira-Blade hits only.
- Missed or interrupted attack attempts do not advance the counter.

Vira-Liquid:
- Pools remain on the arena floor until the next Vira Eruption.
- Liquid pools are harmless before they harden.
- Pools do not move, block movement, or alter fighter collisions.
- Overlapping pools remain mechanically separate but visually blend
  together into a larger irregular mass.

Vira Stacks:
- Maximum: 10
- Each stack represents one active Vira-Liquid pool created by the
  basic attack's two-hit effect.
- The current stack count is always visible in the HUD.
- Reaching 10 stacks immediately activates Vira Eruption.

Vira Eruption:
- All active Vira-Liquid pools harden at the same time and burst long,
  red-edged spikes outward from their irregular edges.
- Initial Eruption Damage: 145 to an enemy touching a pool when it erupts
- Hardened Spike Contact Damage: 38 every 0.5 seconds
- Hardened Duration: 6.25 seconds
- Hardened spikes extend a shorter distance from the liquid pool edges.
- Contact damage uses the visible hardened center and every individual
  spike's full edge-to-tip segment, not only the original liquid area.
- An enemy may be damaged by multiple separate overlapping spike pools.
- Hardened pools are hazards but do not block fighter movement.
- At the end of the duration, the spikes melt and vaporize.
- Vira Stacks and the two-hit counter reset immediately when the
  eruption begins; DarkLord may create new liquid while old spikes remain.

Finalized Presentation:
- Each slash forms an oversized asymmetric forked Vira-Blade based on the
  supplied reference silhouette, then physically rotates through an
  enemy-facing swipe path.
- The forked branches sit near DarkLord's grip while the long killing point
  projects outward toward the enemy.
- Vira-Blades have black bodies, thick dark-red outlines, jagged inner
  cuts, red electricity crawling between their branches, and impact particles.
- Every second hit has a stronger impact frame as liquid splashes outward
  beneath the enemy.
- Before eruption, all pools pulse red once in quick succession.
- On eruption, pools snap solid, grow jagged edge spikes, throw dark
  fragments upward, and release a short localized camera shake.
- Melting spikes visibly collapse, sag, and drip into widened black fluid
  before evaporating into smoky black-and-red particles.


SKILL 1: VIRA-PORTAL AMBUSH
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 9 seconds, beginning when the teleport activates
Trigger: Automatically activates on DarkLord's next wall collision while
the skill is ready

Teleport Behavior:
- On wall collision, DarkLord places a Vira-Liquid portal on that wall
  and is immediately swallowed by it.
- DarkLord becomes untargetable and cannot attack during the transition.
- After 0.45 seconds, a second portal grows at the corresponding position
  on the opposite arena wall.
- DarkLord crawls out of the second portal and immediately dashes toward
  the enemy's position at the moment he emerges.
- Both portals melt and vaporize after use.

Ambush Dash:
- Dash Speed: 2.5x default movement speed, approximately 875 pixels
  per second
- Dash Direction: Locked toward the enemy's snapshotted position
- The dash continues until DarkLord hits the enemy or an arena wall.
- DarkLord cannot basic attack or cast Dark Energy Fire Ball during
  the dash.

Enemy Hit:
- DarkLord forms two Vira-Blades and stabs into the enemy.
- Damage: 217
- DarkLord and the enemy are locked together in place for 1 second.
- DarkLord releases the enemy after 1 second and immediately returns to
  normal movement at default speed.
- The enemy remains Stunned for 1 additional second, for 2 seconds total
  from the initial stab.
- Stun prevents normal movement and starting new attacks, skills, passive
  actions, or other actions. Anything already deployed continues and finishes.

Wall Miss:
- The dash ends when DarkLord hits a wall.
- DarkLord is Immobilized for 2 seconds, then resumes normal movement at
  default speed.
- Immobilize prevents movement but still permits basic attacks, skills,
  passive actions, and normal cooldown progress.
- Anything deployed before Immobilize, including forced movement, continues
  and finishes normally.

Finalized Presentation:
- The entry and exit portals are large wall-bound liquid rifts with layered
  rotating rings, animated tendrils, branching electricity, and jagged edges.
- The entry portal splashes up the wall and folds over DarkLord like a
  liquid mouth.
- The exit portal pushes outward before DarkLord's hands and blades tear
  through it.
- The ambush dash uses broken liquid particles and branching red electricity
  trails without static straight-line speed rails.
- A successful double stab creates twin red impact lines, strong hit-stop,
  and a restrained camera shake.
- During the one-second double-stab lock, violent red electricity continuously
  wraps around and bridges between DarkLord, both embedded blades, and the enemy.
- A missed dash makes DarkLord strike the wall, briefly flatten, drip
  Vira-Liquid, and visibly struggle free during Immobilize.


SKILL 2: DARK ENERGY FIRE BALL
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooldown: 8 seconds, beginning immediately after cast
Requirement: At least 1 Vira Stack
Projectile Count: Equal to DarkLord's current Vira Stacks, from 1 to 10
Stack Rule:
- The current Vira Stack count is snapshotted when the skill casts.
- Casting does not consume Vira Stacks or remove Vira-Liquid pools.

Cast Behavior:
- DarkLord briefly opens a small Vira portal above himself and launches
  the snapshotted number of Dark Energy Fire Balls in a rapid fan.
- The center projectile aims at the enemy's position at cast time.
- Additional projectiles spread evenly to both sides of the center aim.
- Projectiles travel in straight lines and do not home after launch.
- Projectiles disappear when they hit an enemy or arena wall.

Projectile Stats:
- Damage: 42 per Fire Ball
- Speed: Approximately 520 pixels per second
- Radius: Approximately 24 pixels
- Launch Interval: 0.07 seconds between projectiles
- A single enemy may be hit by multiple Fire Balls from one cast.

Finalized Presentation:
- The portal forms as a rotating black disc with a red burning rim.
- Fire Balls are large irregular black-red energy masses with animated flame
  shells, layered inner cores, orbiting sparks and rings, branching electrical
  tails, trailing energy fragments, and smoky particles.
- Higher-stack casts make the portal larger and intensify its glow.
- Each projectile produces a compact dark-energy burst on impact.


PASSIVE: VIRA-SPIDER MIGRATION
------------------------------------------------------------
Recharge: 20 seconds, beginning when a wave spawns
Creatures Per Wave: 6 Vira-Spiders
Damage: 55 per spider hit

Spawn / Movement Behavior:
- Six Vira-Spiders crawl out from several Vira-Liquid portals distributed
  along the arena walls.
- Each spider chooses a different destination point on another wall and
  travels toward it in a straight line at approximately 300 pixels per
  second.
- Their routes are not aimed directly at the enemy.
- A spider damages the enemy once if its body touches them while crossing.
- Each spider may hit the enemy only once per wave.
- Spiders do not block movement and cannot be attacked or destroyed.
- On reaching the destination wall, each spider crawls into a small
  Vira-Liquid portal, which then melts and vaporizes.

Finalized Presentation:
- Wall portals appear in a staggered sequence before clawed black-and-red
  Vira-Spiders pull themselves into the arena.
- Each Vira-Spider follows the reference silhouette: a dark-red vertical
  oval core, two hooked lower claws, and four extremely long arcing legs
  outlined in bright red.
- Spiders have layered black-red bodies, bright eyes, articulated jointed
  legs, hooked claws, small electrical discharges, liquid droplets, and
  broken red movement trails.
- A spider hit creates a red claw-mark impact and briefly splashes apart
  before reforming and continuing toward its destination.
- The six destination portals disappear one after another as the spiders
  finish their migration.


STATUS DEFINITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
Stun:
- Prevents normal movement and starting new attacks, skills, passive actions,
  or other actions.
- Already-deployed actions and effects continue and finish normally.

Immobilize:
- Prevents normal movement only.
- Attacks, skills, passive actions, and cooldowns continue normally.
- Already-deployed actions and forced movement continue and finish normally.

COMPLETED PROTOTYPE SYSTEMS
------------------------------------------------------------
- DarkLord.py is a standalone autonomous showcase against DummyBot.
- Vira-Blade Slash, its two-hit counter, pools, stacks, eruption damage,
  hardened contact damage, melting, and immediate stack reset are implemented.
- Vira-Portal Ambush supports opposite-wall travel, aimed dash, successful
  double-stab lock, enemy Stun, wall-miss crash, and self-Immobilize.
- Dark Energy Fire Ball snapshots the current stack count and fires the
  matching number of straight projectiles without consuming stacks.
- Vira-Spider Migration creates six independently routed wall-to-wall spiders.
- The HUD shows health, ten Vira Stack indicators, status labels, cooldowns,
  and floating combat text.
- All supplied audio files are loaded from DarkLord-SoundEffects.
- The prototype uses Prototype-Shared/visuals.py for the exact established
  MoroZhar arena, 3D ball presentation, movement trails, fonts, and
  DummyBot punch animation.
- DarkLord-specific visuals use staged anticipation, active, impact, and
  recovery presentation instead of single-frame placeholder geometry.


PROTOTYPE NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------
- Combat is fully autonomous.
- DarkLord's Vira-Liquid pools and hardened spikes are arena-floor effects
  and do not alter movement collision.
- Skill 2 intentionally does not consume Vira Stacks, allowing the basic
  attack system to reliably reach Vira Eruption.
- Vira-Portal Ambush uses the opposite arena wall because the standalone
  rectangular prototype has no meaningful wall thickness to travel through.
- Run DarkLord-Prototype/DarkLord.py to watch the completed matchup.
- Headless long-run verification confirmed repeated portal, projectile,
  eruption, spider, Stun, and Immobilize cycles without runtime errors.
- Major visual states were individually rendered and inspected after the
  shared-presentation rework.
- A targeted collision test confirmed that an enemy outside the original
  pool area but touching a visible spike tip receives contact damage.
- DarkLord has a registered adapter in the root main.py tournament runtime
  and can autonomously fight other registered eligible characters.
